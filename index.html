Our Service

Wedding Planning

A full service wedding planning package. We will meet with you at the very beginning and be by your side through the reception. This package ensures that your dreams will come true without worry or stress. You will be a guest at your own wedding.

Actual Day Coordination

Our wedding planning coordinator will manage the entire day, any questions from vendors will come to us and every detail will be seen. You will have the time needed to visit with all of your guests and dance the night away with friends and family.

Wedding Decoration

We are your wedding designer and stylist to work on the stationery design and floral decorative aspects of your wedding and collaborate with you to the feel that is uniquely yours!
